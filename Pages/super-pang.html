Project Overview

Context

Super Pang (Phaser Edition) is a web remake of the classic arcade title. The project focuses on reproducing the feel of the original while maintaining clean, modular JavaScript structure.

Goals

  • Recreate core mechanics with reliable collisions.
  • Maintain responsive input and consistent timing in browser.
  • Use a modular approach for scalability and readability.

Gameplay & Design Focus

Core Mechanics

  • Ball splitting and increasing complexity.
  • Positioning and risk management under pressure.
  • Arcade pacing and difficulty escalation.

Design Pillars

  • Consistency: predictable physics and collisions.
  • Readability: clear hitboxes and player feedback.
  • Performance: avoid spikes with pooling and clean updates.
