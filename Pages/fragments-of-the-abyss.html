Project Overview

Context

Fragments of the Abyss is a survival horror project set in a cursed mansion where tension is built through environmental storytelling, controlled pacing, and reactive threats.

Goals

  • Create tension through anticipation and spatial design.
  • Build encounters that feel reactive and readable.
  • Support fast iteration for level scripting and pacing tweaks.

Gameplay & Design Focus

Core Experience

  • Exploration under pressure and uncertainty.
  • Environmental cues that guide player decisions.
  • Threats designed to create anticipation and vulnerability.

Design Pillars

  • Atmosphere first: lighting, audio, spatial composition.
  • Pacing control: peaks and cooldowns.
  • Readability: clear signals for danger and safe zones.

Technical Implementation

Architecture

  • AI/encounter logic (patrol, activation, response).
  • Interaction framework for environmental objects.
  • Lighting-driven tension moments (timed reveals).
