Project Overview

Context

Magichi is a casual mobile game focused on short sessions, clear objectives, and satisfying progression. The player completes “orders” given by a master, earning rewards to level up and unlock new challenges.

Goals

  • Build a clear and addictive short-session gameplay loop.
  • Deliver responsive mobile-friendly controls and UI.
  • Support scalable tasks and progression without rewriting systems.

Gameplay & Design Focus

Core Loop

  • Receive an order (objective).
  • Perform actions to complete it (mini-task).
  • Get rewards and level up.
  • Unlock new orders and repeat.

Design Pillars

  • Clarity: the player always knows what to do next.
  • Feedback: actions have instant readable response.
  • Progression: rewards drive motivation and retention.
